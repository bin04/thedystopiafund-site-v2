# thedystopiafund-site Intent

The site is the public or semi-public expression of TheDystopiaFund.

It is not merely a news aggregator. It should feel like a machine-mediated commentary system where news, interpretation, and generated visual artifacts combine into something closer to art than stock media.

## Aesthetic Direction

Prefer:

- symbolic inference over literal illustration
- ambiguity, mood, and metaphor
- mundane scenes made uncanny
- images that feel interpreted rather than merely illustrated
- recurring motifs tracked deliberately

Avoid:

- generic cyberpunk defaults
- stock-dystopia visual tropes
- repetitive image motifs caused by weak prompting
- treating generated images as decoration only

Needs human input:

- Desired tone.
- Initial content types.
- Accessibility and readability constraints.
- Visual identity references.
